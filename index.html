HKBuddy – Project Webpage

A Trusted Marketplace for International Students in Hong Kong

1. Project Overview

HKBuddy is a campus-based e-commerce platform designed for international students in Hong Kong. It focuses on solving high-friction problems such as housing search and second-hand transactions through a verified peer-to-peer system.

2. Problem Statement

  • Housing search relies on unreliable social media groups
  • High risk of scams and misinformation
  • Second-hand trading lacks trust and structure
  • No standardized service for remote housing viewing

3. Our Solution

  • Verified student-only marketplace
  • Housing viewing services by senior students
  • Escrow-style payment protection
  • Standardized service and transaction workflow

4. Core Features (MVP)

4.1 Housing Viewing Service

  • Service booking system
  • Standardized inspection checklist
  • Photo & video evidence submission
  • Review and rating system

4.2 Second-hand Marketplace

  • Item listing (electronics, furniture, study tools)
  • On-campus transaction system
  • Graduation gown rental/resale
  • Short-term academic tool rental

5. Target Users

  • Incoming international students
  • Current students relocating
  • Graduating students selling items
  • Senior students providing services
