Project Overview

HKBuddy is a verified peer-to-peer marketplace designed to help international students in Hong Kong solve housing search and second-hand trading problems through a trusted ecosystem.

Core Features

Housing Viewing

Book verified students to inspect properties and provide real feedback with photos and videos.

Second-hand Marketplace

Safe and structured platform for buying and selling student goods.

Escrow Payment

Funds are protected until transaction completion, reducing fraud risk.
